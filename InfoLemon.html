Lemon Spin

(Mar 2020)
This was my first time trying out my ps1 ish low poly pixel art style. I had done low poly before, but never using pixel art textures, and never rendered at a super low resolution. The good results I got from this wound up influencing the style I used in later pieces a lot.

    Tools used:
  • - Unity
  • - Aseprite
  • - Blender
